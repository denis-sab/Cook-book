STEAMED SALMON ON A BED OF SPINACH





                                                         

Non Vegetarian

360 Calories

4-6 Portion



INGREDIENTS

  4

     

Portions


  1 kg           

Leaf spinach


    

1               

Onion

        
    

2              

Cloves of garlic


1 spoon     

Salt


2 tbsp       

Butter


150g         

Salmon fillet


4 tbsp     

Crème fraîche


100ml     

Vegetable broth




How to make a STEAMED SALMON ON A BED OF SPINACH step by step

    Steps

  1. 1

    2. Clean, wash and shake spinach dry. Peel onion and garlic and chop finely. Heat the fat in a pot and sauté the onion and garlic for about 3 minutes until translucent. Add the spinach and let it wilt in the closed pot. Season with salt and nutmeg

  2. 2

    3. Wash the lemon with hot water, grate dry and cut into slices. Wash the salmon, pat dry and season with salt and pepper. Spread the spinach and salmon in a greased baking dish. Mix the crème fraîche, broth and white wine and pour into the tin. Top salmon with lemon slices

  3. 3

    4. Cook in a preheated oven (electric stove: 200 °C/fan oven: 175 °C/gas: level 3) for 15-20 minutes. Bread tastes good with it.
